04 — DJ Set

Pulse Alignment

DJ Set · Live Performance · Beat Matching

Beat matching is the invisible architecture of a great dance floor. Pulse Alignment was conceived as a live demonstration of cross-genre seamlessness: a 75-minute set moving from 88 BPM lo-fi soul through jazz-house, afro-funk, and into 130 BPM techno — without a single audible transition.

The technical challenge was not tempo but feel. A 90 BPM soul track carries a lazy pocket that a 90 BPM tech-house track does not. Matching bars is easy; matching groove requires ear training honed over years. Each bridge in this set was hand-crafted: EQ carves, low-end swaps, harmonic blending, and carefully timed filter sweeps.

Performed live at three venues and then reconstructed in the studio for release, the recording was mastered for both club systems and headphone listening — a rare dual-format approach that preserves both the physicality and the intimacy of the original performance.
