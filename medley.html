01 — Cultural Project

Heritage Collage

Cultural Project · Archive Audio · Medley

A medley is the art of weaving — taking disparate melodies and breathing them into a single, breathing whole. Heritage Collage began as a commission to honour three generations of a family's musical history: a grandmother's folk lullabies, a father's protest songs, and the pop sensibilities of a contemporary daughter.

Working from archival cassette recordings, VHS audio rips, and fresh studio sessions, the challenge was tonal: how do you honour the rawness of 1960s folk while respecting modern listener expectations? The answer lay in careful key modulation, slow harmonic bridging, and strategically placed silence as punctuation.

The final 9-minute piece moves through seven distinct musical themes, each given space to breathe before being gently drawn toward the next — like rivers meeting at a delta. It premiered at a national cultural heritage exhibition and has since been used in a documentary short.
