08 — Drama Series

Echoes Between

Drama Series · ADR & Cleanup · Dialogue

Dialogue editing is the invisible craft. When it's done well, no one notices. Echoes Between is a 6-episode Filipino drama series shot across three locations with production schedules that prioritized picture over sound — a common reality, and one that requires post-production to build what was lost on set.

ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement) accounted for approximately 30% of the finished dialogue. Each session required the actor to re-inhabit performances recorded weeks prior, often in emotional states difficult to reconstruct in a neutral studio. The challenge: making studio-dry ADR sit naturally inside the ambient roar of a crowded marketplace or the intimacy of a candlelit bedroom.

iZotope RX's Dialogue Match and Ambience Match tools were essential in bridging the sonic gap between location sound and studio recordings. The finished series broadcast nationally and received a Television Academy nomination for Best Sound in Drama.
