City Pulse Vol. 2

Live Event · Club Set · Mashup

City Pulse Vol. 2 is the second in a series of live mashup performances designed for urban, mixed-demographic audiences. Where Vol. 1 focused on era-bridging (50s jazz over 2010s beats), Vol. 2 explored cultural collision: OPM ballads fused with UK drill, Bossa Nova rhythms beneath trap hi-hats.

Fourteen source tracks were licensed, stems extracted, and processed individually before the live performance. The set was built in Ableton with careful attention to key compatibility — using the Camelot Wheel to ensure harmonic coherence across wildly different genres. Where keys clashed, pitch-shifted acapellas were used as neutral bridges.

The live audience reaction was the most reliable metric: the moment when two songs that seemingly have no business being together suddenly snap into perfect sense. That moment — that 'oh' of recognition — is what this work chases.
